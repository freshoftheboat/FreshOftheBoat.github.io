The court

  • The court is a rectangle, length = 59 ft; width = 29.5 ft
  • The net is 7 feet for 11-12s girls
  • 10 feet from the net on either side of the court is called the 10 foot line and the lines that mark the end of the court are endlines. The lines that border the sides are sidelines.

actions

  • Postions and zones

    • 6 zones on the court:rightback, rightfront, middlefront, leftfront, leftback, and middleback (1,2,3,4,5,6)

      • Postitions + more

    • 12 players on the team. 6 people on the court
    • backrow/main passers(right, middle, leftback)
    • libero = best passer in the team. usually wears a different color jersey, can have up to 2 liberos
    • setter = usually gets 2nd touch and sets anybody on the court so they can attack. only 1 setter on the court
    • hitter = attacks by hitting, tip, roll-shots, set it over, freeball, and downballing. outside, middle, and opposite or oppo are all hitters. back row attacks are made by backrow players and they cannot step over the 10-foot line when downballing
