The court

  • The court is a rectangle, length = 59 ft; width = 29.5 ft
  • The net is 7 feet for 11-12s girls
  • 10 feet from the net on either side of the court is called the 10 foot line and the lines that mark the end of the court are endlines. The lines that border the sides are sidelines.

Postions and zones

  • 6 zones on the court:rightback, rightfront, middlefront, leftfront, leftback, and middleback (1,2,3,4,5,6)
